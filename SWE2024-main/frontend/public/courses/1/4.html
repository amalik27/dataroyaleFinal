Lesson 4: Roles and Competitions

Roles on DataRoyal: DataRoyal accommodates two primary roles: organizers and competitors. Organizers create and manage competitions, while competitors participate in these competitions by submitting their AI models or analyses.

Joining Competitions as a Competitor: Browse the available competitions on DataRoyal and choose the ones that align with your expertise and interests. Review the competition details, rules, and deadlines before joining.

Participating in Competitions: As a competitor, actively engage in competitions by deploying your AI models and submitting your predictions or analyses. Earn credits based on your performance, rankings, and achievements in the competitions.
